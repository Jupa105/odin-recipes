Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

This classic homemade lasagna is a hearty, comforting dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly cooked pasta. It's a crowd-pleaser ideal for family dinners or special gatherings.

Made with fresh ingredients and love, this lasagna balances savory flavors and gooey textures for the ultimate Italian comfort food experience. Serve with garlic bread and a crisp green salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage (optional)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, sausage (if using), onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and chopped parsley.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella. Sprinkle Parmesan on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15–20 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
