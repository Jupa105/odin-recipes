Mouth-Watering sStrawberry Cheesecake

This strawberry cheesecake is a creamy, indulgent dessert with a buttery graham cracker crust and a rich cream cheese filling. Topped with a sweet strawberry glaze and fresh berries, it's a crowd-pleaser for any occasion.

Perfectly balanced between tangy and sweet, this cheesecake melts in your mouth and offers a refreshing burst of strawberry flavor in every bite. Serve chilled for the best experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup strawberry jam or sauce
  • Fresh strawberries for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then let cool.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla extract and mix well.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, making sure each is fully incorporated before adding the next.
  5. Mix in the sour cream until smooth, then pour the filling over the cooled crust.
  6. Bake for 55 to 70 minutes, or until the center is almost set. Let cool in the oven with the door cracked for 1 hour.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Top with strawberry jam or sauce and fresh strawberries before serving.
